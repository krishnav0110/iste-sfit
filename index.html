ISTE SFIT WELCOMES YOU TO ITS WEB PORTAL!

The SFIT Student Chapter of the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) was founded in 2005, and is one of the oldest Student Committees of the Institute. Under the able guidance of Prof. Bysani, the 2019 iteration of the SFIT ISTE Committee is here to provide a multi-faceted experience to students of all branches, in all kinds of technical domains right from web development to artificial intelligence. ISTE regularly conducts seminars, workshops, and competitions propelling students forward in the fields of their preference.
